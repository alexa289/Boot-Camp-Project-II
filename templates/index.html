Wine Reviews

Project II

ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Our data is all about wine. The data was obtained from Kaggle.com, and consists of 130,000 wine reviews from Wine Enthusiast magazine. The data set was designed to offer some opportunities for sentiment analysis and other text related analysis. Our other data set was scraped from Binnys, and includes their selection of wines.

WHAT'S IN OUR DATA?

The wine review data includes country and province of origin, variety, winery, price and points of a wine, along with a review / description. The Binny’s data answers the question – is the wine I want available at Binny’s?

WHY WE CHOSE IT??

Because data on whisky wasn’t readily available! Actually, the wine data was a very large, and varied set of information. It offered the chance to analyze the data, and develop an application to search the data, and to offer a chance to interact with another data set (Binny’s)

WHY IS NOTEWORTHY?

The number of varieties of wine is immense. Finding the wine or wines that are of interest is difficult. Once you have found a wine of interest, buying it can also be a challenge. Our project was to make the wine finding process easier!

HOW WE CLEANED OUR DATA?

Initially 129971 reviews -> Removed Duplicated Descriptions -> Removed Empty Price ->Removed empty varieties -> Removed Empty Countries -> Final Dataset == 111511 entries -> 691 varieties -> 42 countries -> over 15k wineries
