We wanted to see if there was a significant correlation between price and points given in the wine reviews. We found out that even if the linear model is not as good, R-squared < 0.5, there's a significant correlation between the cost of wine and its rating, namely theres an average $1.05 increase for every one point incrase in rating. By itself, this would not be a good predictive model.
